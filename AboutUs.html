About:

Hey there, hope you all liked this app. This web application is my own project fully designed by me.
I have created it out of scrach using HTML, CSS and Bootstrap for Front-End UI/UX and tried
to keep it responsive, simple & user friendly so that there is not any complexity during use.
JS has also been used for making DOM interactive. Database is designed using MySQLi supported
by PHP; also I have started its intigration using Cloud FireStore Database which would be realtime
and faster, the Queries? section is implented using that only. Hope you like it..

Usage:

Creating a quiz:
Step 1: Click on PROCEED of Create quiz.
Step 2: Enter Title of quiz/test in Header, duration in minutes(by default 48 hours), No of questions.
Step 3: Press SUBMIT.
Step 4: Write the questions in the text area and if require put an image with given requirements.
Step 5: Press SUBMIT.
Step 6: Note the Quiz Id, it will be important for your quiz takers to enter quiz.

Now, your quiz/test has been created, share your mail id and quiz id with your students.

Usage:

Attempting a quiz:
Step 1: Click on PROCEED of Attempt quiz.
Step 2: Enter quiz id provided by examiner and his email.
Step 3: Press SUBMIT.
Step 4: Write the answers in the text area and complete every answers.
Step 5: Press SUBMIT.
Step 6: See for the message after submission for confirmation of your successful attempt.

Now, wait for your marks to be available on My Marks section.

Usage:

Answer script evaluation:
Step 1: Click on EVALUATE.
Step 2: Enter quiz id. You can only view answer scripts of your Quiz.
Step 3: Press SUBMIT.
Step 4: By default everybody is marked -99, click on SEE>> to see the anwser script
Step 5: Check the answers corresponding every answer number and after whole evaluation mark them.
Step 6: Press SUBMIT.

Step 7: See for marks updation alert!
Step 8: Now proceed the same way to check the rest.

After you have updated the marks it will now reflect in the My Marks section of the Quiz takers
